Prefab Cottages - Single Bed

PREFAB COTTAGES | 9 x 7 ft
Single Bed | 3 x 6 ft

Designed with the solo traveler in mind, our Prefab Single Cottages offer a peaceful and private space to unwind. Compact yet thoughtfully laid out, these container-style cottages provide all the essentials for a comfortable stay. Inside, you'll find a cozy single bed perfect for restful nights, along with an ensuite bathroom for added privacy and ease. A private verandah extends your living space outdoors — ideal for enjoying a morning coffee or soaking in the stillness of your surroundings.

The room also includes practical touches like a ceiling fan, closet/storage space, and a simple, calming interior that invites rest and reflection. Whether you're traveling for work, a solo retreat, or just passing through, our Prefab Single Cottages offer the perfect balance of comfort, quiet, and convenience.

Max capacity per room is 2

HB Hotels from personal dream to luxury hotel

"Welcome to HB Safari Lodge, where adventure meets comfort in the heart of Uganda’s stunning wilderness. We offer more than just a place to stay – we provide unforgettable experiences. Our expert tour guides lead you on thrilling journeys to Murchison Falls National Park, where you can get up close with Uganda’s iconic wildlife on exciting game drives and boat safaris along the Nile. At HB Safari Lodge, we also specialize in team building retreats, providing an inspiring setting for groups to connect, innovate, and recharge

Enjoy premium accommodation in a serene environment where you can relax against the backdrop of a lush avocado orchard, the shimmering waters of Lake Albert, and sunsets that light up the sky with golden hues. Discover the perfect blend of adventure, comfort, and natural beauty at HB Safari Lodge – your gateway to extraordinary experiences in Uganda.”.
