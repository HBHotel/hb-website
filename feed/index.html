    • A TWO NIGHT FUN-FILLED EASTER ADVENTURE

    Embark on our annual springtime adventure with Easter Escapades, an all-inclusive package designed to celebrate the most magical time on the farm and our estate. Dive into our age-old family traditions and create unforgettable memories with a day filled with joyful activities.

    Easter on The Farm: A Day of Simple Joys & Family Traditions

    Start your day with a gourmet breakfast served overlooking the breathtaking Valle Serena. Amidst this scenic backdrop, savour the aroma of freshly brewed coffee and delight in hand-painted Easter eggs adorning your table. At 10:30, kick off the excitement with our Easter Egg Hunt. Our treasure hunt has rapidly become an annual tradition not to be missed. The search starts at the villa of Borgo Santo Pietro and takes you through its 13 acres of gardens, parks and more! Each guest will receive a bespoke basket and a card to collect 5 distinct eggs and solve the mysteries concealed within to win exciting prizes.

    Don’t forget to take a moment to relax and discover the hidden ‘Bunny Droppings’ at each location to enjoy a glass of our Per gli Amici Rosé or refreshing beverages amidst the verdant beauty. If you’ve solved all the riddles, grab your baskets full of treasures and head to our Trattoria Sull’Albero to collect your winnings!

    A Farm-to-Plate Easter Lunch

    After the thrilling escapades, gather with your treasures at our Green Michelin Star Saporium Restaurant for a delectable 5-course Easter Lunch. A menu curated to reflect the traditional and wholesome essence of this day. For our young guests, an extra treat awaits with a special pizza-making class. Watch as they create their culinary masterpieces and relish in the joy of enjoying their freshly baked creations for lunch – truly irresistible!

    The Package Includes:

    • 2 Night Accommodation with daily gourmet breakfast served overlooking Valle Serena or via in-room service

    • Complimentary Welcome Amenity including an Easter treat

    • Complimentary Easter Egg Treasure Hunt on Sunday morning in the park and grounds, with treats along the way

    • 6-Course gourmet Easter Sunday lunch
       for 2 served at Our Green Michelin Star Saporium Restaurant

    • Pizza-making class for the children, followed by the enjoyment of their freshly baked creations

    • Fun-filled activities

      for youngsters

    • Borgo SPA: Choice of one Seed to Skin Tuscany Face & Body Rituals 105’, among four treatments, per person

    Create lasting memories with your loved ones and immerse yourself in the spirit of Easter Escapades. Join us for a two-night adventure filled with laughter, joy, and cherished traditions.
