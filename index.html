HB Safari Tour and Travel

Discover Uganda’s natural marvels with HB Tour and Travel. Murchison Falls National Park is a breathtaking destination where the mighty Nile squeezes through a narrow gorge before cascading into a spectacular waterfall. Home to diverse wildlife—from elephants and giraffes to hippos, buffaloes, and elusive predators—this park promises an unforgettable safari experience. Conveniently located just 15 km away, HB Safari Bulisa offers an ideal alternative to staying within the park, ensuring you’re only a five-minute drive away from nature’s wonders.

At HB Tour and Travel, we go beyond just providing a place to stay. Our seasoned guides take you on thrilling game drives, allowing you to experience Uganda’s iconic wildlife up close, and organize captivating boat safaris along the Nile for unique views of Murchison Falls. Whether you’re in the mood for a half-day adventure or a full-day expedition, our tours are designed to deliver a perfect blend of excitement and comfort. Let us craft an extraordinary travel experience that brings you face-to-face with the raw beauty of Uganda’s wilderness, all while enjoying the comfort and convenience of HB Safari Bulisa.

HB Safari Sports Turf

HB Safari Lodge’s outdoor sports turf is a versatile and well-maintained facility designed to enhance your outdoor activity experience. Spanning 38 meters in length and 26 meters in width, this dynamic space offers a smooth, safe playing surface perfect for both casual and competitive sports. Whether you’re into small-sided football games with clearly marked boundaries, basketball on adjustable half or full courts, or rugby practice with ample room for running drills, our turf is equipped to meet your sporting needs. In addition, a dedicated 140-meter by 2-meter running track provides an ideal space for jogging or sprinting, while the flexible area can also accommodate volleyball, badminton, and various fitness training sessions.

Accessible to all lodge guests for a nominal fee, the sports turf ensures you have everything you need to stay active during your stay. Basic sports equipment, including balls, nets, and markers, is available, with additional amenities available upon request. To make the most of this facility, reservations can be easily made through the lodge’s reception or our dedicated reservations team—advanced booking is recommended during peak seasons. With regular inspections and diligent maintenance, our outdoor sports turf offers a safe, engaging environment for guests to enjoy a wide range of recreational activities. ,  .
